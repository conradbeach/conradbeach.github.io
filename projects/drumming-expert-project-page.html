Front-end Redesign of DrummingExpert.com

DrummingExpert.com is a site designed to teach anyone how to play drums. I redesigned the front-end using Foundation 5 for a framework. This was my first attempt at writing all the code for a site from scratch. I learned a lot, and I learned to love the Foundation framework. Props to the guys over at Zurb.

Built With: HTML5, CSS3 and Foundation 5

Date: April 2014 (some pages have yet to be completed)
