Let's Get Started! How to Access and Make the Most of These Free Lessons:

Drumming is a "Mastery of Motions"

Drumming is a very physical activity and to succeed you must master the physical motions required. Much like, golf, tennis, or martial arts; there is a "vocabulary" of physical motions used to play drums. In drumming we call these "strokes."

Each of the following lessons flow step by step through this "stroke vocabulary" that will help you develop incredible skills as a drummer.

There are really only three basic rudiments in drumming:

  • Single Stroke Roll
  • Double Stroke Roll
  • Flams

All other rudiments are combinations of these three "categories" of drumming. Yes, there are a few exception, but those are easy to deal with once you have mastered these three.

Lesson Three: Mastery of Motion - The "Stroke Development Sequence"
The is like the "Tia-chi" of drumming - programming your hands for success. Here we begin training your muscles in the exact motions and strokes required to be an amazing player - the best way to play drums online.

Section I: Single Strokes - Let's Drum!

Lesson Four: Single Strokes One Stick Height - Let's Drum!
It has taken three lessons to lay an incredible foundation, but we are moving our hands and on our way. In this lesson you will apply the strokes you have learned to playing single stroke rhythms with both hands.

Lesson Five: Rhythm Reading and Tempo Control
Mastering the basic rhythm vocabulary of drumming is huge. This lesson will reveal the secrets of how to develop rock solid time and groove!

Lesson Six: Single Strokes Two Heights - Separating the "Men from the Boys!"
Here you will learn a powerful aspect of drumming that most players never even get to. If you want to rise above the average drummer, this is where you do it! Now you will develop ability to control two different stick heights and create very cool patterns and grooves. Master this, and you will enter a whole new realm of drumming.

Lesson Seven: Rebound Stroke (Single Stroke Roll) - "I Have a Need for Speed!"
We have spent six lessons developing smooth effortless wrist motion and control of the sticks. It is now time to learn how to "let go" and allow the sticks to fly to great speeds.

Section II: Double Strokes - Diddles and Rolls

Lesson Eight: Buzz Rolls - How to make a drum "Sing"!
In this lesson we will learn how to play lush smooth buzz rolls to create awesome textures of sustain and sound. When learning to play drums online or any other way, this is a key to becoming a well rounded drummer.

Lesson Nine: Double Stroke Rolls - Separating the "Men from the Boys"
We will develop the skill to play fast powerful diddles and double-stroke rolls. This takes chops!

Lesson Ten: Paradiddles, Drags and Accented Rolls
Combining the world of Accent Patterns and Double-Strokes gives you the ability to play very advanced rudiments and create very cool licks for any musical setting.

Section III: Flams

Lesson Eleven: The Magical World of Flams!
If you are serious about learning to play the drums, flams will make your skills complete. They are quickly becoming a lost art. As you study the lessons to play drums online, you will become an expert at one of the most unique and spectacular drum rudiments in existence. - Very few drummers learn how to play this stuff!
